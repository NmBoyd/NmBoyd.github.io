About Me

Hello

I'm Nathan Boyd, a passionate Robotics engineer who has worked with various mechatronic systems such as Mobile Robots, Drones, Robot Arms, and Legged Robots. My main interest is the study of legged robots and manipulation for complex environments. Typically I don't do research in specific algorithm development and instead try my best to solve the "system" problem. I'm working toward a M.S at Georgia Tech in Mechanical Engineering with a focus in Robotics.
